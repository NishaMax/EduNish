Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 23, 2025

This page explains how EduNish collects, uses, and protects your information.

Who we are

EduNish is a student-focused practice and quiz website for Grades 10–11. For questions, contact us at nishantha0426@gmail.com

Information we collect

  • Account details: Email, full name, grade, and a generated Student ID.
  • Usage data: Quiz attempts, scores, selected lessons/grades, timestamps.
  • Device data (basic): Browser type, language, and limited technical data your browser provides.
  • Cookies & local storage: We use browser storage to keep you signed in and remember your progress.

How we use your information

  • To create/maintain your Student ID and profile.
  • To deliver quizzes, track results, and show progress.
  • To improve the site’s performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To communicate important updates (e.g., feature changes or policy updates).

Legal bases (if applicable)

We process information to perform our service to you (providing quizzes and progress tracking) and based on our legitimate interests in improving EduNish. Where required, we obtain your consent (e.g., non-essential cookies).

Cookies & local storage

EduNish primarily uses your browser’s local storage to save your Student ID and quiz progress. We may use essential cookies for security and session management. You can clear cookies/local storage in your browser settings, but some features may stop working.

Analytics & third-party tools

Currently: EduNish does not use advertising or share data with advertisers. If we enable analytics or other tools in the future, we will update this policy and list those providers here, including what data they collect and why.

Children’s privacy

EduNish is designed for students in Grades 10–11 and is intended to be used with the awareness of a parent/guardian or teacher when required by local law. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has provided personal data without your consent, contact us to remove it.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers that help us operate EduNish (e.g., hosting), under contracts that require them to protect it. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights or users’ safety.

Data retention

We keep your account and quiz data for as long as you use EduNish. You can request deletion of your Student ID and associated data; after deletion, we may retain minimal records as required by law or for legitimate interests (e.g., security logs) for a limited time.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, download, or delete your data, or to object to/limit processing. Contact us at nishantha0426@gmail.com to make a request.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International transfers

Your data may be processed in other countries. Where required, we implement safeguards for cross-border transfers.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by posting an update on this page or within the site.

Contact us

Questions or requests about your privacy? Email nishantha0426@gmail.com
